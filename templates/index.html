FixYouTube

Embed YouTube videos on Telegram, Discord and more!

FixYouTube serves fixed YouTube video embeds. Heavily inspired by fxtwitter.com and ddinstagram.com.

How to Use

Replace www.youtube.com or youtu.be with this domain to fix embeds for short videos.
